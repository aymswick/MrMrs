Jen + Jon

theme

"Two Become One" Our wedding was held on August 21 and we thought of a creative way for our guests to remember our wedding on our save the date and wedding invitation! 2 Shall Become One! After 4 years of marriage, Our wedding theme keeps us grounded in our Faith and love for each other.

 

favorite

Our favorite moment from our wedding is when we first laid eyes on each other while walking down the aisle. It was a moment of sheer joy and excitement where we realized how blessed we were to be marrying each other!

 

advice

Enjoy every moment of wedding planning! It can be quite busy, but in the midst of all of the planning, never forget the reason why you are getting married and your deep love for each other!
