theme

Rustic. DIY. Antique.

 

favorite

Saying our hand-written vows to each other in front of our friends and family, and dancing for three straight hours to the best band ever: Sky Hi!

 

advice

Enjoy your engagement and don't rush the wedding. It's fun to call each other your fiancé for awhile! Don't sweat the small stuff when planning your wedding. Focus on two or three things that really matter to you, and spend the majority of your time and money on those things. For us, it was our photographs, music, food and beverages! Yes, we had amazing flowers and a beautiful cake, but what we wanted people to remember most was what an awesome party we threw and how much fun we had! All of the other details will fall into place, trust us!
